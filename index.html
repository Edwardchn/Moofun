About MOOFUN

MOOFUN is a **Web3 mini-game** built on Telegram and the **TON blockchain**. Players can join the **Spin & Win** game, earning rewards through strategy and luck.

The game introduces **multiple ways to earn rewards**, including **daily spins, challenges, and a referral system**. Players can invite friends to get **extra in-game bonuses and virtual currency rewards**.

Game Features

Web3 Competitive Game on TON

Play within Telegram, no downloads needed. Fully decentralized, all assets tradable on-chain.

Diverse Game Modes

Spin-to-win strategy & luck. Daily challenges, leaderboards, and KOL prize pools.

Reward System

Earn daily chests, $TON rewards, ecosystem incentives, and KOL dividends.

Social Economy Model

Invite friends to earn rewards. Global KOL prize pools, marketplace, and token incentives.
